Our Financial Services

Simple, secure, and designed for your convenience.

Daily Savings Collection

Our core service is designed for the active business owner. A dedicated agent will visit your business daily to collect your savings, ensuring your earnings are deposited securely without interrupting your work. Every transaction is recorded in real-time on your online dashboard.

Hassle-Free Withdrawals

Accessing your money should be simple. Request a withdrawal through your online dashboard, and your assigned agent will contact you to arrange a convenient and secure cash delivery.

Mobile Money Integration

For added flexibility, you can make deposits using Mobile Money (MTN, Orange). Simply log into your dashboard, indicate the amount sent, and provide the transaction reference number. Our administrative team will then verify the transaction and credit your account.
