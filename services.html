Our Financial Services

Simple, secure, and designed for your convenience.

Daily Savings Collection

Our core service is designed for the active business owner. A dedicated agent from Unity MFI will visit your place of business daily to collect your savings. This ensures your earnings are deposited securely without interrupting your work. Every transaction is recorded in real-time on your online dashboard.

  • ✔ On-location collection for maximum convenience.
  • ✔ Instant digital record of every deposit.
  • ✔ Build your savings securely, day by day.

Hassle-Free Withdrawals

Accessing your money should be simple. With your Unity MFI account, you can request a withdrawal through your online dashboard. Once requested, you can coordinate with your assigned agent to receive your funds at a convenient time and location. Our goal is to make your funds accessible when you need them.

  • ✔ Initiate withdrawal requests online.
  • ✔ Coordinate directly with your trusted agent.
  • ✔ Secure and verified transaction process.

Mobile Money Deposits

For added flexibility, you can make deposits using Mobile Money (MTN, Orange). Simply log into your dashboard, indicate the amount you are sending, and provide the transaction reference number. Our administrative team will then verify the transaction and credit your account. It's a convenient way to add to your savings anytime.

  • ✔ Log deposits from anywhere.
  • ✔ Secure manual verification by our team.
  • ✔ Keep your savings growing even outside business hours.
